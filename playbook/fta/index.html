Framework — 7 Feb 2026

United States

Interim trade-agreement framework released. Full negotiations on hold pending US tariff-policy clarity. Watch for digital-services and pharma resolutions.

Launched — 2 Mar 2026

Canada (CEPA)

Formal negotiations re-launched after a 2023 pause. Strong potential in critical minerals, education services, and pulses trade.

Active

Peru

Negotiations ongoing. Targeting Latin-American market gateway with focus on minerals, agri-products, and pharmaceuticals.

Active

GCC (Gulf Cooperation Council)

Bloc-level negotiations exploratory. Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain remaining; UAE and Oman already covered bilaterally.

Exploratory

Israel

Long-stalled negotiations. Defence-tech, water-tech, and agri-tech cooperation potential.

Pending

BIMSTEC FTA

Bay-of-Bengal regional pact (Bangladesh, Bhutan, India, Myanmar, Nepal, Sri Lanka, Thailand). Goods framework long-pending finalisation.
