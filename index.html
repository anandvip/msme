§ 00

Welcome, Future Agri-Entrepreneur

This document captures the complete foundational journey for any agriculture-based revenue generation system. Whether your final form becomes crop-based, value-added processing, agritech services, organic inputs, farm-to-consumer, or something entirely new — this is your practical base.

§ 01

Register a Proprietorship Firm + Udyam Aadhaar

Step 1: PAN & Aadhaar Ready
Ensure you have PAN card and Aadhaar linked with mobile number.
Step 2: Udyam Aadhaar (MSME Registration) — Do This First
Official Link: udyamregistration.gov.in
• Completely free • Takes 10–15 minutes • Instant certificate with QR code
Benefits: Priority loans, subsidies, government schemes, easier bank account opening.
Step 3: Current Bank Account in Firm Name
Use Udyam Certificate + PAN + Aadhaar + Address Proof.
Step 4: Shop & Establishment Registration (State-specific)
Search “[Your State] Shop and Establishment Online Portal”
Step 5: GST Registration (if turnover > ₹40L / ₹20L)
Portal: gst.gov.in
§ 02

Udyam Aadhaar – Quick Start Guide

Why do it immediately? It gives your agriculture startup instant credibility and access to dozens of government schemes designed for agri-MSMEs.

  • One registration for multiple activities (farming, processing, trading, services)
  • No renewal required
  • Self-declared investment & turnover
§ 03

Businesses Involving Regulated Sectors

Food & Beverage

  • Food processing, organic products, spices, ready-to-eat, dairy, honey, pickles
  • Restaurants, cloud kitchens, FPO products
  • Required: FSSAI License

Agriculture Inputs & Chemicals

  • Fertilizers, pesticides, bio-stimulants, seeds
  • Required: Fertilizer License, Insecticide License, Seed Certification

Healthcare / Nutraceuticals (Agri-linked)

  • Ayurvedic herbs, medicinal plants, organic supplements
  • Required: AYUSH / Drug Controller approvals

Import-Export of Agri Commodities

  • Rice, spices, fruits, vegetables, coffee, tea
  • Required: IEC Code + APEDA + FSSAI (for food)

Manufacturing & Processing Units

  • Oil mills, flour mills, cold storage, packaging, bio-fertilizer plants
  • Required: Factory License, Pollution NOC, Fire NOC
§ 04

Next Actions (Blank State → Revenue)

  1. Complete Udyam + Bank Account this week
  2. Decide core revenue model (contract farming, value-added products, agritourism, input supply, tech platform, export, etc.)
  3. Validate idea with 20–30 potential customers/farmers
  4. Prepare simple one-page business model
  5. Apply for relevant licenses based on chosen sector
  6. Explore subsidies: PM-KISAN, Agriculture Infrastructure Fund, NABARD, State schemes
