§ 01

Legal Foundation — Proprietorship + Udyam

Start here. These steps create the legal shell for any agriculture activity (inputs, processing, services, horticulture, export, FPO-linked, etc.).

Immediate Action: Complete Udyam Aadhaar today — free, instant, unlocks subsidies & banking.
  • Udyam Registration → udyamregistration.gov.in (10–15 mins, instant PDF with QR)
  • Current Bank Account in firm name (use Udyam certificate)
  • Shop & Establishment Registration (state labour portal)
  • GST Registration (if turnover crosses threshold or inter-state)
§ 02

Financial Architecture — Ground Study Reference

Three-instrument structure + layered schemes deliver low-cost capital for post-harvest, processing, or value-add agriculture projects.

Loan Instruments

Term Loan — Fixed assets (chambers, civil, machinery)
CC Limit — Working capital (procurement, operations)
Bank Guarantee — Tenders & credibility

Spine Scheme

Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)
Up to ₹2 Cr • Effective ~6% • Collateral-free (CGTMSE)

Reference Case: Aurick Hisar (Banana Ripening Centre) — funded via NABARD ABIC at HAU Hisar. Same state, same stack, recent & operational.
§ 03

Scheme Stack — Decision Tree

  • AIF (Spine) — 6% effective
  • NHB / NABARD AMI — 33–35% capital subsidy
  • PMEGP / PMFME — 25–35% margin money
  • Haryana State Subvention — Additional up to 6%
